This graph shows the performance of vessels over time, distinguished by whether or not they should have sailed. Such an opportunity is defined as a continuous period of at least {{MinContinuousWorkingHours}} hours in which Hs stayes below the max accepted wave height. This limit can be changed below. Any opportunity must occur during working hours ({{WorkdayStartTimeHours}}-{{WorkdayStopTimeHours}}).
